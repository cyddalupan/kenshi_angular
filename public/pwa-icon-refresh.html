📋 Manual Steps by Browser

🌐 Chrome / Edge / Brave

1 Open chrome://apps
2 Right-click "ToyBits Skeleton" → Remove from Chrome
3 Open chrome://serviceworker-internals
4 Find "pwa.toybits.cloud" → Click Unregister
5 Visit https://pwa.toybits.cloud/
6 Click Install button or browser menu → "Install app"

🍎 Safari (iOS/Mac)

1 Delete app from home screen (tap & hold → Remove App)
2 Open Safari → Settings → Advanced → Website Data
3 Find "pwa.toybits.cloud" → Swipe left → Delete
4 Visit https://pwa.toybits.cloud/
5 Tap Share button (⎋) → "Add to Home Screen"

🦊 Firefox

1 Open about:preferences#privacy
2 Click Clear Data (Cookies and Site Data)
3 Check "Offline Website Data" → Clear
4 Visit https://pwa.toybits.cloud/
5 Click hamburger menu → "Install ToyBits Skeleton"
